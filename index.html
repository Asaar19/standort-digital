Imapsync Online Unlimited

Copy/synchronize a complete Mailbox to another, without duplicates!

free of charge

Use our free mailsync online tool to transfer email mailboxes. The necessary traffic is also included free of charge.

save traffic

With one click, the tool copies all data between two IMAP servers. This will save you all the traffic that would be generated by an intermediate copy on your server.

worldwide

The tool works worldwide without restrictions, regardless of the location of the IMAP server.
